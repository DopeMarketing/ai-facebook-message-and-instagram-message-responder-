# AI Facebook message and Instagram message responder 

## Overview
An AI-powered platform that enables business owners to create seasonal product knowledge bases through self-interviews and deploy intelligent chatbots across social media channels to handle customer inquiries automatically. The system learns from the owner's expertise to provide personalized, brand-consistent responses to leads and customer service requests.

## Problem
Business owners struggle to consistently respond to customer inquiries across multiple social media platforms, especially during busy seasons, leading to lost leads and poor customer experience

## Target User
Small to medium business owners who offer seasonal products/services and manage their own social media customer interactions

## Features
- Self-interview system for seasonal product knowledge capture
- Multi-platform social media chatbot deployment
- Lead qualification and response automation
- Customer service inquiry handling
- Brand voice customization and training
